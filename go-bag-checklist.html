// Canine — 4yr American Akita

Micco

Akitas are a working breed known for stress-triggered reactivity. Preparation and management are critical at shelters and around other animals.

⚠ Breed-Specific Shelter Warning Many emergency shelters do NOT accept large/reactive breeds. Pre-identify pet-friendly hotels (101 corridor: Willits, Ukiah, Santa Rosa) and boarding facilities before an emergency. Keep vaccination records on your phone and in Micco's bag.
