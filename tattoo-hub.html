💡 Pro Tips
Line Weight
Lines thinner than 1mm will blur and spread within 5–10 years. Fine line work needs careful placement away from high-flex areas like wrists and elbows.
Color Retention
Blacks and dark blues hold longest. Yellows and pinks fade fastest. White ink rarely stays true — often turns yellowish. UV exposure accelerates all fading.
Detail Density
Crowded small details merge over time. A rule of thumb: if you can't clearly see each element with a pencil at the final size, it'll muddy in skin.
Placement
Hands, feet, knees, and elbows heal poorly due to constant movement. Inner bicep and ribs are high-pain. Outer upper arm and thigh heal best.
Stencil Size
Always print and tape the stencil to the skin before committing. Clients consistently underestimate how large a tattoo needs to be to look right.
