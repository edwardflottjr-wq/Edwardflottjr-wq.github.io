Vehicle Emergency Kit

Your Jeep is also an evacuation platform and a survival base if you're stranded on Hwy 1 or Hwy 20. Coastal terrain, redwood routes, and single-road access make vehicle prep non-negotiable in this region.

⚠ Fort Bragg Road Hazard Reality Hwy 1 and Hwy 20 are both subject to closure during wildfires, mudslides, earthquake damage, and flooding. Hwy 1 has no alternate in many sections. If both primary routes close simultaneously (happened during the 2018 fires), you may be sheltering in your vehicle for 12–48+ hours. Plan accordingly.
🔧 Jeep Advantage If yours is a 4WD-capable Jeep, you may have off-road egress options others don't — but only if you have recovery gear and know the terrain. Don't count on going off-road in a wildfire or post-quake scenario unless you've pre-scouted the route.
